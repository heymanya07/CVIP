Complete list of temperature conversion formulae

  • 1 Celsius [°C] = 274.15 kelvin [K]
  • 1 Fahrenheit [°F] = 255.9277777778 kelvin [K]
  • 1 Celsius [°C] = 33.8 Fahrenheit [°F]
  • C = (F − 32) × 5⁄9;  C = K − 273.15
  • F = C(9⁄5) + 32;  F = (K – 273.15) × 9⁄5 + 32
  • K = C + 273.15;  K = (F − 32) × 5⁄9 + 273.15
